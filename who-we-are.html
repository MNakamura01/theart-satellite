What is the Satellite project

The Satellite project is a web site created to connect people, through the arts, which are vehicles, used from the former to the present, as means of exposing feelings, thoughts, sciences among other things. The users of this site will have how to put their works on the web site.

What is our purpose?

Our purpose is to keep people connected in this time of pandemic and social isolation,and through art create a way for them to continue to express themselves and bring reflections and inspiration to people.
